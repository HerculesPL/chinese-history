A Civilisation Unbroken

China's story stretches across five millennia — the longest continuous civilisation on Earth. Michael Wood traces it not just through emperors and armies, but through the voices of peasants, poets, merchants, and women who shaped one-fifth of humanity. Scroll through each dynasty to explore its world, and click each person to hear their story.
